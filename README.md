Project Brief: ACADEMON
- Is a web-based educational platform that transforms traditional studying into an engaging, game-inspired learning experience. Inspired by monster-battling games, Academon motivates students to review lessons, practice their knowledge, and improve academically through interactive battles, AI assistance, and a rewarding progression system.

- Instead of simply reading notes or answering quizzes, students become trainers who strengthen their knowledge by battling subject-based opponents. Every victory contributes to their overall progress through experience points (XP), badges, and win streaks, making learning both enjoyable and motivating.

- Academon also integrates artificial intelligence to provide students with instant academic assistance across multiple subjects. Combined with a personal note management system and multilingual support, the platform serves as an all-in-one study companion for learners.

Objectives
- Make studying more engaging through battles and AI.
- Encourage students to review consistently using rewards and progression.
- Provide accessible AI assistance for various academic subjects.
- Help students organize their study materials efficiently.
- Create an enjoyable environment that motivates continuous learning.

Interactive Dashboard
- Badges Earned
- Win Streak Counter
- Trainer Level (XP System)
- Quick Battle Access
- Personal Notes
- AI Review Assistant

Battle Based Learning
- One of Academon's core features is its Pokémon-inspired battle system.
- Students battle enemies by answering educational questions instead of relying on chance alone. Different battle categories correspond to various academic subjects, making studying interactive and rewarding.
The battle system includes:
- Turn-based gameplay
- Subject-based challenges
- Move selection interface
- Battle progression
- Win/Loss outcomes
- Learning through repetition

Smart Notes
Academon includes a built-in note management system where students can:
- Create study notes
- Organize notes by subject
- Add titles and content
- Translate notes between supported languages
- Easily review lessons before entering battles

AI Professor (Review Bot)
Academon's AI-powered Professor acts as a virtual tutor capable of assisting students across multiple academic subjects.
Supported subjects include:
- Mathematics
- Science
- English
- Filipino
- History
- Programming
- General Questions

The AI assistant can
- Answer academic questions
- Explain difficult concepts
- Assist with assignments
- Clarify lesson topics
- Provide learning support anytime

Badges/Achievements
Academon keeps students motivated through an achievement-based progression system.
Students earn rewards by actively using the platform, including:
- Badges for accomplishments
- Win Streaks for consistent victories
- Experience Points (XP)
- Trainer Levels

Traditional studying can often feel repetitive and unmotivating. Academon addresses this by combining educational content with game mechanics that keep students engaged.
